BELLA NOTTE — BOOKING & RESERVATION POLICY
==========================================
Last updated: April 2026

MAKING A RESERVATION
---------------------
Reservations can be made by telephone, via our website, or by speaking directly with our booking agent.
We accept reservations up to 30 days in advance.
Same-day reservations are welcome subject to availability, however bookings made less than 2 hours
before the requested dining time cannot be guaranteed and we recommend calling the restaurant directly
or walking in.

CONFIRMATION
-------------
All reservations receive a confirmation reference number in the format BN-XXXX-2026.
Please retain this reference as it will be required if you wish to amend or cancel your booking.
Email confirmations are sent automatically to the address provided at the time of booking.
If you do not receive a confirmation within 30 minutes, please contact us on +1-800-555-0199.

CANCELLATIONS & AMENDMENTS
----------------------------
We kindly ask that you provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or amend your reservation.
Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice for tables of 1–5 guests: no charge, however repeated
late cancellations may affect future booking privileges.
Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice for tables of 6 or more guests: a cancellation fee of
£25 per person may apply if a valid credit card was provided at the time of booking.
To amend or cancel a reservation please call +1-800-555-0199 or email reservations@bellanotte.com
and have your reference number ready.

DEPOSITS & CREDIT CARD GUARANTEES
------------------------------------
Groups of 6 or more guests are required to provide a valid credit card to secure the reservation.
No charge is made to the card unless the cancellation policy (above) is triggered.
For private dining and exclusive events, a deposit of 25% of the minimum spend is required at the
time of booking. Deposits are fully refundable with 72 hours' notice.
Friday and Saturday Tasting Menu bookings for 4 or more guests require a credit card guarantee.

LATE ARRIVALS
--------------
We hold tables for a maximum of 15 minutes beyond the reserved time.
If you are running late please contact us as soon as possible on +1-800-555-0199 so we can do our best
to accommodate you. We cannot guarantee your table will be held beyond the 15-minute grace period
during busy service periods.

DIETARY REQUIREMENTS & ALLERGIES
----------------------------------
We are committed to accommodating a wide range of dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan,
gluten-free, nut-free, and other allergen requirements.
Please inform us of ALL dietary requirements and allergies at the time of booking so our kitchen
can prepare accordingly.
Our menu features clear (V) Vegetarian, (VG) Vegan, and (GF) Gluten-Free indicators.
While every care is taken, our kitchen handles all major allergens including nuts, gluten, dairy,
eggs, shellfish, and fish. We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
Please also notify your server on arrival of any dietary requirements so they can advise accordingly.

DRESS CODE
-----------
Smart casual attire is requested. We ask that guests refrain from wearing sportswear, flip-flops,
or gym wear. Jackets are welcomed but not required. We reserve the right to decline entry to guests
dressed inappropriately.

CHILDREN
---------
Children are warmly welcomed at Bella Notte. High chairs are available on request — please mention
this when making your reservation. A children's menu is available upon request. We ask that children
are supervised at all times for the comfort of all our guests.

OUTSIDE FOOD & BEVERAGES
--------------------------
Guests are welcome to bring their own celebration cake for a small cake cutting fee of £3.50 per
person. Outside wine or Champagne may be brought with the agreement of the restaurant management and
is subject to a corkage fee of £25 per bottle. No other outside food or beverages are permitted.

PRIVATE DINING & EVENTS
-------------------------
We offer exclusive use of our private dining room (capacity: up to 20 guests) for celebrations,
corporate dinners, and special events. A minimum food and beverage spend applies.
Please contact our events team at events@bellanotte.com or call +1-800-555-0199 for details and
availability.
